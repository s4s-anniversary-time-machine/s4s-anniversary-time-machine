Yosho's /s4s/ Anniversary Time Machine

When the spikeman burnt down the web hosts for foolzashit and worldathleticproject, virtually all early history (before November 2013) of /s4s/ was lost.

This is just a small collection of archived 2013 /s4s/ pages and threads, in particular from the evening of April 1st, and an early mention of Bury Pink on August 18th.

The majority of these come from the Yotsuba Society archiving project, which I only recently found out had all these s4s threads. From them, you can download all the sources I used for pages hosted on this website. Note that, at least for s4s, the 3gb 7zip archive has the same content as the 18gb tarball.

The earliest Bury Pink link I've found is here (post number 809411). This comment contains the image "1370645975351.png", which is unix time for June 7th, 2013. This is very strong, but not irrefutable, evidence that the image truly was uploaded on June 7th and contained Bury. I find it unlikely but not impossible that this anon doctored the filename only to fool us 10 years later.

I hope anons find these links entertaining, but you should save your own firsthand sources. I will shut down this website in one month.

The established s4s timeline can be found on the fandom wiki.
